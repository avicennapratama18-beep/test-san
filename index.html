Eau Rouge

A legendary blind crest taken completely flat-out. The sheer vertical climb forces immense G-loads on both car and driver, creating one of the most iconic sights in the world of racing.

Kemmel Straight

Following the steep climb, drivers unleash maximum power down the endless Kemmel Straight. Aerodynamic efficiency and raw engine output are tested to their absolute limits here.

Pouhon

A rapid, double-apex left-hander requiring utter commitment. Pouhon is a true test of a car's downforce and a driver's courage, separating the extraordinary from the rest.

Blanchimont

A sweeping, high-speed left curve before the final bus stop chicane. Hitting the apex perfectly at maximum speed is an exhilarating rush unique to the Ardennes landscape.
